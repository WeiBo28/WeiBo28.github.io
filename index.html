BIO

I am a senior Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University at Buffalo, under the supervision of Prof. Wenyao Xu, with a focus on high-impact real-world applications of innovative Internet-of-Things (IoT). My research specifically targets the fields of healthcare, smart health, embedded/mobile sensing, and related intelligent analytics/computing, aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical data analysis and practical implementation to improve efficiency, safety, and accuracy.
